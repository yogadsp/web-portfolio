Sistem Manajemen Dokumen ICOSITER

The International Conference on Science, Infrastructure Technology and Regional Development (IcoSiter) is an annual international conference held at Institut Teknologi Sumatera. In its implementation, there is a journal collection procession to the committee. This activity raises a problem when conducting a journal recap by the committee. The web-based Simadok Icositer is here to solve this problem with the following features:

  • Adding a journal.
  • Change journal information.
  • Delete journal.
  • Sorting journals.
  • Adding an IcoSiter activity gallery.
